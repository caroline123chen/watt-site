Where the money goes钱花在哪了

Most of an AI bill isn't new work. It's waste you're paying frontier prices for.AI 账单的大头不是新工作，而是你在用前沿模型的价格为浪费买单。

Re-sent context重复发送的上下文

Your agent re-reads its whole history every loop, so you pay for the same tokens again and again — often the single biggest line in the bill.Agent 每一轮都把完整历史重新读一遍，于是同样的 token 被反复计费——往往是账单里最大的一项。

Frontier prices for easy tasks简单任务用了前沿模型的价

Formatting, extraction, and boilerplate run on a top-tier model when a far cheaper one would finish them identically.格式化、抽取、模板化这些活儿都跑在顶级模型上，其实更便宜的模型也能做到一样的结果。

No routing on outcome没有按结果路由

Existing routers pick a model on price and latency — none check whether the task actually got done, so you can't safely route down.现有的路由只看价格和延迟，不判断任务是否真的完成——所以你不敢放心往更便宜的模型上切。

How Watt optimization worksWatt 优化怎么工作

Four steps. Your stack never changes.四步。你的技术栈完全不用动。

1

Classify分类

Each request is scored — reason vs. execute, hard vs. boilerplate — to decide what can safely route down.为每个请求打分——是推理还是执行、是难题还是模板活——判断哪些可以安全降档。

2

Compress压缩

Redundant re-sent history is stripped before the call is priced, while caches are preserved.在计费之前剥掉重复发送的历史，同时保留缓存。

3

Route路由

Easy work goes to a cheaper model in your approved pool; hard reasoning stays frontier. Automatic failover.简单任务交给你授权范围内更便宜的模型，难推理仍走前沿模型。自动故障兜底。

4

Prove验证

Every call is logged against what it would have cost on the frontier — so savings are auditable, per request.每一次调用都对照“如果走前沿模型会花多少”记账——每个请求省了多少，可逐条审计。

The honest answers把丑话说在前面

Where does my data go?我的数据会去哪？

You control the model pool.模型池由你掌控。

Default to your existing providers only. Add cheaper models when — and only when — you choose. No traffic goes anywhere you didn't opt into.默认只用你现有的供应商。要不要接入更便宜的模型，完全由你决定。任何你没主动选择的地方，都不会收到流量。

Is my data used to train you?会拿我的数据训练吗？

No. Your prompts are yours.不会。你的 prompt 是你的。

Watt learns routing from metadata — task type, model, cost, whether it passed your quality check — never your prompt or output content.Watt 只从元数据学习路由——任务类型、模型、成本、是否通过质量校验——绝不碰你的 prompt 或输出内容。

If Watt goes down?Watt 挂了怎么办？

Failover to the model directly.直接兜底到模型本身。

A thin proxy with automatic fallback: if anything fails, the call goes straight to your provider. An outage degrades to "no savings," never "no service."一层很薄的代理，带自动兜底：一旦出问题，调用直接打到你的供应商。故障最多让你“省不到钱”，而不会“服务中断”。

Will it work with my stack?能接进我的技术栈吗？

OpenAI-compatible. One base-URL change.兼容 OpenAI 接口。改一个 base URL。

Streaming, tool calls, and structured outputs pass through unchanged. If your agent speaks the OpenAI API, it speaks Watt.流式、工具调用、结构化输出原样透传。只要你的 Agent 用 OpenAI 接口，就能直接接 Watt。

What does optimization cost?优化怎么收费？

A share of verified savings.只从可验证的节省里抽成。

You see your measured baseline and the per-request savings first. We keep a slice (~25–30%) of verified savings; your net bill still drops. No savings, no fee.你会先看到实测基线和每个请求的节省。我们只从已验证的节省里拿一份（约 25–30%），你的净账单仍然下降。不省钱，不收费。

How do you judge quality?怎么判断质量？

You set the floor.底线由你设。

Cheap-routed outputs are scored against a bar you control. Anything below it promotes back to the frontier automatically — start so high that nothing routes down until you trust it.被路由到便宜模型的输出按你设定的质量线打分。低于这条线的自动切回前沿模型——你可以先把底线设得很高，在信任之前一个都不降档。
